The Recording Setup

We use the rigs showed in the following image which consists for three Event Cameras and a High resolution RGB camera and the data collection is performed on an Intel NUC.

Prophese Cameras

  • Resoultion: 640 x 480
  • Note: There are two prophese event cameras set up as a stereo pair.

Samsumg Monocular Camera

  • Resoultion: 640 x 480
  • Note: This camera is at the center of the rig.

Flea Camera

  • Resoultion: 2080x1552
  • Note: This camera is at the center of the rig.
