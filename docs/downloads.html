    The EVIMO2 Dataset

    The Recording Setup

    We use the rigs showed in the following image which consists of three Event Cameras and a high resolution RGB camera. The recorded data is stored on an Intel NUC.

    Prophesee Cameras

    • Resolution: 640×480
    • FOV: 71.27°
    • Note: There are two Prophesee event cameras set up as a stereo pair.

    Samsung Monocular Camera

    • Resolution: 640×480
    • FOV: 75.47°
    • Note: This camera is at the center of the rig.

    Flea Camera

    • Resolution: 2080×1552
    • FOV: 64.35°
    • Note: This camera is at the center of the rig.
